v2.0.4 "Harbor" - Android release candidate.

The cycle-accurate emulation core is unchanged from v2.0.3 (AccuracyCoin 141/141, 100%). This build focuses on release readiness:

This is a testing-track candidate while we verify on-device stability before the public launch.
