## Accuracy honesty

The v2.0.1 AccuracyCoin re-sync grew the catalog to 146 rows / 141 assigned tests,
adding two new upstream PPU tests — **"ALE + Read"** and **"Hybrid Addresses"** —
that exercise the octal-latch address-corruption timing when `$2007` is touched
mid-render. The core measures **139/141 (98.58%)**: those two are known gaps. Two
bounded fixes were attempted this release — Option 2 (`mc-ppu-bus-addr-hybrid`,
Mesen2's persistent `_ppuBusAddress` model, #234) and Option 1 (a 2-cycle-ALE
fetch refactor, #236) — and **both reach only 139/141 flag-on**, empirically
confirming (ADR 0030) that closing these needs a dedicated Timebase-scale campaign,
not a bounded fork. Both ship default-off; the shipped build is byte-identical at
139/141. **The full fix is the headline accuracy work of the v2.0.2–v2.0.4 cycle.**

## foss/play flavor split (structural start, ADR 0025)

The `distribution` flavor dimension landed with a default **`foss`** flavor (no
Google SDKs / ads / tracking — the F-Droid + sideload artifact) and a **`play`**
flavor. The five proprietary subsystems (Billing, Cast/Chromecast, Play Games v2,
Integrity, in-app update/review) moved to `playImplementation` behind `src/main`
façades with no-op `src/foss/` twins and real `src/play/` implementations —
including the CAF `MediaRouteButton` (routed through the `ChromecastSender` façade
so `src/main` links no `androidx.mediarouter` / `com.google.android.gms.cast`).
Both `assembleFoss*` and `assemblePlay*` build green in CI. Monetization stays
dormant; on-device flavor verification is deferred to v2.0.9.
